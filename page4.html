Professional Players

  • Optic Nadeshot

Matthew Haag, (born August 3, 1992) also known as NaDeSHoT, is a professional American Call of Duty player currently on hiatus from competitive gaming. He is the co-owner and former captain of the OpTic Gaming Team. Haag is a Major League Gaming (MLG) and X Games gold medalist. He won the best e-sports player at The Game Awards int 2014. He is a Red Bull eSports athlete and participates in MLG.TV network which is a streaming program. He is also sponsored by several gaming hardware companies, including Astro Gaming and Scuf Gaming.

  • Faze Censor

Doug Martin, (born August 21, 1994) or better known as "Censor" in gaming. He plays for FaZe Red in the United States. He is currently the captain of the FaZe squad. He won the MLG National Championship 2011 for Call of Duty Ghost in Providence, Rhode Island. He was a player for Strictly Business that is a known team that has earned lots of rewards but then he left the team and joined FaZe, he has been the captain of the team since then.
